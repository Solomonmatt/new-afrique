About Mere Afrique

Mere Afrique is inspiring everyone around the world, and giving adequate opportunities to read/ study Africa as a book or course and to experience Africa in close proximity as much as possible.

Mere Afriqueis the first ever of its kind. However the goal of this organization is to completely focus on africa , that hapens to be the new found wealth of the world. Mere Afrique is for entertainment and not just entertainment alone but also to inspire everyone who is prepared for the next vital stage in Africa's history. At Mere Afrique there are no boundaries, reasoning cut across everyone.

The Adventure(Africa by Road)

An opportunity to explore the untried, the unknown and the unimagined. This is a rare drive overland round Africa (Africa in circle). This adventure is daring and at the same time exciting, participants/ participation has no limits. You could have woken up in Freetown,Liberia today and be having dinner in Dakar,Senegal at night.Africa by road opens up ability to deal with any challenges head on; this adventure prepares and gives one confidence that problems can always be solved with minimal preparation. Participants learns that one must have intellectual principles and guide post , but at the same time one must be very flexible to accommodate sudden changes . Changes in weather,human character, altitude,food,language,drive side,tribes,mind set,challenges,comfort etc . Imagine setting out with other 11 individuals in about 6 SUVs to navigate between 33 to 38 African Countries in terrain you have never been to and probably not even heard about all your life. Driving over the deserts with the heatwave stirring you in the face; hoping not to run out of water or fuel .Could even be at the safari with the lion king jumping all over your SUV roaring at you? Or even competing with your fellow participants and placing a bait on who gets to the top of Mountain Kinimajaro first? Or even creating your own Dakar rally at the desert. The rate and depth of this adventure is up to the individual,
I Mayowa Adebola enjoin everyone to enter for this new venture challenge as we re position Africa (the new found wealth of the world)with personal opportunity to win seed money.

Please Note: This adventure is opened to everyone around the world for selection without regard to race,color,religion,sex,national or ethnic origin, disability, profession or other factors irrelevant to participate. But you have to be atleast 18 years old to participate
